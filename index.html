Who Am I?

My name is Rachel Espinoza. I am a student, a parent, a wife, and trying to graudate from UCR with my degree in Biology. When the pandemic closed California down on March 16, 2020- I really assumed that my life would take a bit of a pause. I never would have imagined that a shutdown of everything "normal" would be the momentum for so much change in my life. My husband proposed (and, more importantly, I said "yes") on our anniversary in September of 2020. Almost a year to the date after that, I quit my career so that I could focus on community college... and found out a few weeks later that I was pregnant with my son.
Well, 4.5 years, 3 moves, 2 toddlers, and 1 goal in mind later- here I am! I'm looking forward to updating this page as I continue practicing code, but for now- this is who I am.
